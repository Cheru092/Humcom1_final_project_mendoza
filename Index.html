Come and eat with us, welcome to the cookbook from another universe! this website aims to create an enviroment which aspiring chiefs can use in order to access varying recipes from desserts to the most beautiful main dishes. We here aim to show people what we can do with food and how to create food that are both easy to follow as well as make this a codex for food.



This is created by a singular person, this website is a non-profit website and we don't earn money from the this website. All recipes are credited their respective creators.

How do you use this website?

You simply scroll down to the very bottom of the page, once you see the Join us button, click on it and fill the relevant information. Once done simply click on the recipe you want to view and it will redirect you to the appropriate website containing the recipe of your desire.


Why use the website?

This website offers an easier way to gain access to recipes which would take hours or even days to find via conventional means. This website will be updated constantly to include even more recipes.
